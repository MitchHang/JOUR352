Mitchell Hang
Amateur archivist at work.
Mitchell Hang is an amateur archivist who enjoys working with old media formats. As a child, Mitchell grew to love watching television and playing with the features on his family's video cassette recorder. Now, he specializes in converting VHS videotapes to digital formats, in addition to other interests related to broadcasting. He is currently enrolled as a journalism major at the University of Maryland in College Park. He describes himself as "a team-centered individual" who is highly self-motivated, a quick learner, and can adapt to enjoy working in any welcoming environment, in addition to other interests related to media and broadcasting. He is currently working on designing webpages using GitHub Desktop, Bootsrap and Atom as part of an interactive design and development class at the University of Maryland. Eager to learn at every available opportunity, Mitchell Hang is able to collaborate with other team members on projects, plan ahead of time, and treat everybody with kindness.
